Great Smile Changes Everything

Welcome to village Dental, your locally owned, patient-centered dental office in Kennesaw, GA, committed to the health and well-being of our patients. We take pride in fostering a supportive, dynamic, and passionate team culture that allows us to utilize our personalities and talents to deliver extraordinary care in a friendly environment. We’ll make sure you understand everything from treatment procedures to costs, so all you have to worry about is how to show off your new smile. As your local Kennesaw dental office, we’re proud to positively impact our community and improve our patients’ day-to-day lives by restoring optimal dental function, easing pain, and treating others by the golden rule. We also offer Spanish language assistance to help alleviate any potential barriers to optimal care. At our practice, you always come first.
